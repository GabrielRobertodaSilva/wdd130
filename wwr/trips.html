Main River Run

Our most popular trip! This 3-hour journey takes you through the scenic heart of the main canyon. It features moderate Class II rapids, making it perfect for families and first-timers who want a mix of relaxation and fun.

White Water Challenge

For those seeking more adrenaline, the Challenge offers a full day of Class IV rapids. You'll navigate tight drops and massive waves under the guidance of our expert instructors. Lunch is included by the riverbank.

The Canyon Explorer

Go beyond the ordinary with our two-day expedition. Spend the day conquering extreme rapids and the night camping under the stars in a remote canyon. All camping gear and gourmet meals are provided.
